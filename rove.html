Case Study

ROVE - remote online virtual education

My role

Creative Direction
UI Design
Motion Design

Client

Self initiative project

Year

2018

Why?

With daily life becoming increasingly busy, hectic work schedules and lack of time makes education only a secondary priority. Remote workers are becoming more prevalent in today’s business world. Being able to work from anywhere in the world has gradually become a new trend. Also, for some of the family who needs to move often, such as military family and ambassador family. Such abrupt changes interfere with children’s learning, inevitably causing them to fall behind in various curriculums that differ from school to school. To adapt to the change, an alternative would be the idea of homeschooling or online school, however, most parents are too busy or not inept enough to teach their children the core subjects offered at the school.

What?

ROVE is an accredited education system that is designed for the next generation. It utilizes AI technology to translate different languages and assist students immediately. People can use ROVE to learn different languages and culture without facing language barriers. With this virtual classroom, students can access key forms of learning from anywhere at any time.

How?

In order to clarify the direction and main goal of this virtual classroom education system, I started by using mind mapping to find the right keyword for the program. The four keywords that represent ROVE are community, accredited, flexible and pioneer. In addition to creating the visual assets for ROVE, including the website and app prototype, I also managed to interview some school teachers and families who travel often in order to make sure the design direction of ROVE stayed on the right track.

ROVE stands for Remote Online Virtual Education, the pioneer of the virtual classroom for the new generation. Using AI technology, the robot will translate different languages and answer questions immediately. It not only solves the language barrier but also helps to ease the learning process. ROVE provides the learning environment without having the distance, time or language limitations. Where you live, what time zone you’re in, or what language you speak will no longer be a problem. All you need is a personal computer or tablet to access ROVE and connect to other ROVE users.

Conclusion

ROVE allows people to travel anywhere without worrying about missing children’s educational opportunity. ROVE is a malleable, accredited virtual classroom. With ROVE, you will access the class anywhere in the world and communicate with people from all around the world. ROVE brings everyone closer by sharing the same education platform with different country’s people and creates more learning opportunity for people.In summary, the intent of this project is based on both my own personal career goals and experience, as an international student, when I face problems, I always think globally. ROVE is made for people from all around the world.
